HomeInternet Safety Guidelines

By partaking on the internet, in an online community like LIVESTOCK LOADER, you are exposing yourself to safety concerns. Here are some no nonsense tips that we recommend you follow while interacting with people on LIVESTOCK LOADER.

  • Do not share your personal information with people you meet on LIVESTOCK LOADER. Personal information is information that is unique to you and can be used to identify, contact, or locate a person or can or can be used with other available resources to uniquely identify an individual. The following are examples of personal information you should not provide to anyone:
    • Your full name
    • Banking information
    • Home address
    • Phone number
    • Facebook ID or other similar IDs
  • Websites containing the above information, or information regarding your relatives or friends.
  • Do not share your email or instant messaging addresses with people on LIVESTOCK LOADER, the person you share it with may not be the one he or she claims to be and their intentions may not be fully disclosed.
  • Opening files sent by other people on LIVESTOCK LOADER could be potentially harmful to your computer or may not be the type of content that you think it is
  • Do not send personal pictures to people you meet on LIVESTOCK LOADER
  • If you decide to have real life meetings or decide to connect with other members of LIVESTOCK LOADER, we urge you to follow common sense safety precautions, such as:
    • Always let someone you trust know where you are going and when you will be back.
    • Arrange meetings in a public place.
    • Be honest about your gender and age, being dishonest can lead to a potential awkward or dangerous situation.
    • Take threats, bullying, or other illegal activity seriously! If you or someone you know in the LIVESTOCK LOADER community feel unsafe due to the above behavior please immediately report this behavior to LIVESTOCK LOADER. If you feel that criminal activity or a criminal act has or may happen, please report to your local police.
